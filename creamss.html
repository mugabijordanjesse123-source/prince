About Us

Who We Are

Creamalnd Secondary School is a mixed day and boarding institution offering O‑Level and A‑Level education. We provide a safe, disciplined, and nurturing environment where learners can reach their full potential.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures responsible, confident, and academically excellent students, equipped to serve their communities and the world.

Our Vision

To be a top-performing secondary school in Nyakakoni and beyond, known for discipline, innovation, and outstanding academic results.

Message from the Principal

Welcome to Creamalnd Secondary School

Dear students, parents, and visitors,

At Creamalnd Secondary School, we believe that every learner has the potential to excel. Our dedicated team of teachers, supportive non‑teaching staff, and committed administration work together to ensure that each student receives the best possible education.

We emphasize discipline, hard work, and moral uprightness, alongside strong academics and co‑curricular activities. We invite you to join our community as we shape future leaders with integrity and excellence.

Admissions

We admit students into S1–S4 (O‑Level) and S5–S6 (A‑Level).

  • Pick an application form from the school office.
  • Attach recent report cards or UNEB results.
  • Attend an interview or placement test.
  • Receive admission letter and fee structure.

Fees Structure

O‑Level (S1–S4)

  • Tuition: 350,000 UGX per term
  • Boarding (optional): 450,000 UGX per term
  • Uniform (full set): 120,000 UGX
  • Registration & ID: 30,000 UGX

Note: Fees are subject to change. Please confirm with the school bursar for the latest structure.

A‑Level (S5–S6)

  • Tuition: 450,000 UGX per term
  • Boarding (optional): 500,000 UGX per term
  • Uniform (full set): 120,000 UGX
  • Registration & ID: 30,000 UGX

Science combinations may have a small practical fee; details available at the office.
